TDR Advisory

Skyward Urban Solution LLP provides strategic assessment and advisory support for lands affected by reservations under Development Plan 1991 (DP 1991) and DCPR 2034 frameworks.

  • Preliminary evaluation of reservation-related development potential, planning implications, regulatory positioning, and feasibility aspects associated with road reservations, amenity reservations, public purpose lands, infrastructure proposals, and urban planning restrictions.
  • Assessment of applicable planning provisions, DP remarks, land records, CTS and PRC details, reservation status, surrounding development conditions, and potential regulatory pathways relevant to the property.
  • Assistance in understanding possible development opportunities, TDR-related implications, reservation-linked benefits, procedural considerations, and documentation requirements associated with urban planning and municipal processes.
  • Structured strategic guidance and professional support in navigating complex reservation-related land matters within the evolving urban development framework of Mumbai and the MMR region.

Development Potential & Feasibility

Skyward Urban Solution LLP provides preliminary assessment and strategic evaluation of urban land development potential under applicable planning and regulatory frameworks including DP 1991 and DCPR 2034.

  • Feasibility-oriented review of reservation status, planning constraints, development opportunities, access conditions, surrounding urban infrastructure, land configuration, and applicable development control provisions relevant to the property.
  • Review of CTS and PRC records, DP remarks, reservation implications, road setback impact, infrastructure considerations, and potential planning benefits associated with the land.
  • Understanding the possible development outlook, regulatory positioning, urban planning implications, and strategic opportunities associated with the property from a preliminary advisory perspective.
  • Structured and practical approach towards evaluating development possibilities while considering applicable municipal regulations, planning policies, and procedural requirements relevant to urban development matters in Mumbai and the MMR region.
