Company Introduction

Skyward Urban Solution LLP

Skyward Urban Solution LLP is a Mumbai-based strategic urban advisory and development consultancy firm specializing in TDR, reservation lands, urban feasibility, and regulatory development solutions across Mumbai and the MMR region.

The firm assists landowners, public trusts, housing societies, institutions, developers, and industrial property holders in identifying and unlocking the development potential of urban lands affected by reservations, road setbacks, redevelopment regulations, and planning constraints.

Skyward Urban Solution LLP undertakes advisory and strategic assessment related to Development Plan 1991 (DP 1991) and DCPR 2034 reservations, including road reservations, public amenity lands, parking reservations, and urban infrastructure-related development opportunities.

With a multidisciplinary approach combining technical assessment, regulatory understanding, municipal coordination, and strategic advisory, the firm provides structured support for complex urban land matters involving TDR realization, reservation feasibility, CTS and PRC coordination, development potential analysis, municipal processes, and urban planning strategy.

The firm also provides specialized advisory support for public charitable trusts and institutional landowners in matters involving reservation lands, redevelopment potential, TDR entitlement assessment, and regulatory processes connected with urban land monetisation and planning permissions.

At Skyward Urban Solution LLP, we believe that urban land challenges can be transformed into strategic development opportunities through structured planning, regulatory insight, technical evaluation, and integrated advisory solutions.

Approach & Philosophy

Strategy-Driven Urban Land Advisory

At Skyward Urban Solution LLP, we adopt a structured and strategy-driven approach towards complex urban land and development matters. Every assignment is evaluated through technical, regulatory, planning, and feasibility perspectives to identify the most practical and value-oriented development pathway.

Our approach focuses on detailed land assessment, reservation analysis, regulatory understanding, municipal coordination, and long-term development potential evaluation under applicable planning frameworks including DP 1991 and DCPR 2034.

The firm believes in combining technical diligence, regulatory insight, strategic planning, and practical execution support to help stakeholders navigate complex urban development challenges in a transparent and professionally structured manner.
