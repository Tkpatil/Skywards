Nature of Work

What We Undertake

Skyward Urban Solution LLP undertakes strategic advisory, preliminary assessment, documentation support, regulatory coordination, and development-related consultancy services associated with urban land, reservation, infrastructure, and TDR matters across Mumbai and the MMR region.

The nature of work may include reservation assessment, development feasibility evaluation, planning-related advisory, CTS / PRC coordination, demarcation support, documentation review, authority-process coordination, regulatory compliance support, and urban development strategy associated with applicable planning frameworks including DP 1991 and DCPR 2034.

The firm also assists stakeholders in matters relating to road reservations, public amenity reservations, parking reservations, trust and institutional properties, infrastructure-linked development potential, and authority-level procedural processes connected with urban planning and development matters.

Assignments are undertaken through a structured and multidisciplinary approach involving technical review, planning assessment, procedural coordination, and regulatory understanding relevant to the proposed development process.
