Our Process

A Structured, Compliance-First Approach

Every assignment is assessed through technical, planning, regulatory, documentation, and authority-process perspectives to identify a practical and compliant development pathway.

Skyward Urban Solution LLP follows a structured and strategy-driven approach for TDR, reservation land, and urban development matters. Each assignment is assessed through technical, planning, regulatory, documentation, and authority-process perspectives to identify a practical and compliant development pathway.

Our process includes preliminary due diligence, demarcation, title review, joint measurement, documentation support, inter-departmental verification, regulatory coordination, and authority-level processing associated with TDR and reservation-related matters.

For trust or institutional properties, additional regulatory processes including Charity Commissioner approvals and related compliance procedures may also be applicable depending upon the nature of the property and transaction.

The objective of our structured process is to help stakeholders navigate complex urban development and TDR-related matters in a transparent, organized, and professionally coordinated manner.

9-Phase Process

Detailed Process Phases

Phase 01

Preliminary Due Diligence & Technical Verification

Assessment of land records, DP remarks, reservation status, title-related documents, and preliminary feasibility.

Phase 02

Demarcation & Reservation Verification

Demarcation coordination, road alignment verification, and technical assessment of affected areas.

Phase 03

Title Review & Legal Coordination

Preliminary title scrutiny, land record review, and legal documentation coordination.

Phase 04

Joint Measurement & Area Finalization

Joint measurement procedures, subdivision coordination, possession documentation, and area verification.

Phase 05

Documentation & Compliance Process

CTS / PRC coordination, procedural compliances, and technical documentation support.

Phase 06

Inter-Departmental NOCs & Regulatory Verification

Departmental verifications, planning scrutiny, and authority-level regulatory processes.

Phase 07

TDR Assessment & Authority-Level Determination

Planning review, development potential assessment, and preliminary TDR entitlement evaluation.

Phase 08

Charity Commissioner Process

(For Trust & Institutional Properties, wherever applicable) Regulatory processes relating to trust permissions, resolutions, notices, valuation, and legal documentation.

Phase 09

Final Processing & DRC Issuance

Final online processing and issuance of Development Rights Certificate (DRC), subject to authority approvals.

Important Note

The above process is indicative in nature and may vary depending upon property type, reservation category, title position, regulatory framework, and authority-level requirements. While the firm endeavors to provide structured advisory, coordination, and compliance support throughout the process, the final grant, approval, quantum, or issuance of TDR / DRC shall remain solely subject to the approval of the concerned statutory and government authorities.
